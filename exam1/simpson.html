Simpson Family Tree

The following list is a few members and relatives of the Simpson family.

  1. Homer Simpson
    • Homer is my father and married to my step-mother Marge.
  2. Marge Bouvier
    • Marge is the wife of Homer and the mother of Bart, Hugo, Lisa, and Maggie.
  3. Abraham Simpson
    • Abraham is the father of Homer.
  4. Mona Olsen
    • Mona is the mother of Homer.
  5. Orville Simpson
    • Orville is the grandfather of Homer and father of Abraham.
  6. Yuma Hickman
    • Yuma is the wife of Orville.
