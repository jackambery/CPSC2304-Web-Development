Simpson Family Gallery

The following gallery displays numerous images of my father's side of the family, the Simpson family. Click any of the images to see larger, more detailed versions!

Homer Simpson

Homer Simpson is my biological father. He is clumsy, fat and very lazy. Homer is also an alcoholic, and is not very intelligent. He works as a Safety Inspector at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant.

Marge Simpson

Marge Simpson is my step-mother. She is generally a very kind-hearted and loving woman. In contrast to her husband and son, Marge hardly ever swears and, except on occasional incidents, puts greater effort into controlling her temper.

The Simpson Family

Displayed on the right is a picture of the whole family. You can see from left to right, Marge, Maggie, Homer (in the air), Lisa, and Bart.
